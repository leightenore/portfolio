About Me

My name is Leigh Tenore, and I'm a former special education teacher turned web developer. After working in education for six years, I decided it was time for a career change. I had wanted to learn how to code for years after taking a basic Computer Science course in college, so I enrolled in University of Arizona's six month coding bootcamp, and developed a passion for all things tech and coding. Please feel free take a look around and view some of my recent projects. If you have any questions, my contact information can be found on my resume or on the contact page.
